Vehicle editing rules

Vehicle details (type, colour etc.) are user-editable. If you want to get involved, great! But please follow these rules, so you don’t waste your time and others’:

  1. If a vehicle’s tracking as the wrong vehicle (i.e. the “ticket machine code” is wrong), please don’t try to correct it on timesbus.org – instead, wait for the operator to correct their data.
  2. Don’t “remove from list” just because a vehicle’s been advertised for sale, was involved in an accident, or hasn’t tracked for a while.
  3. Don’t update a vehicle’s livery until it’s finished being repainted and you can share some evidence.

  4. Do use the “summary” field to link to some evidence, such as a picture you’ve uploaded to Imgur. But please don’t link to private Facebook groups – private means we can’t see them.

  5. Edit sparingly – you don’t need to “tidy up” up or make everything “consistent”.

Users can report content to us in the discord which they believe to be harmful, illegal, or or otherwise unsuitable. If we deem your content to be harmful, illegal, or otherwise unsuitable, we will permanently delete that content, and may ban your account from adding or editing content.
