Vehicle editing rules

Vehicle details (type, colour etc.) are user-editable. If you want to get involved, great! But please follow these rules, so you don’t waste your time and others’:

  1. If a vehicle is tracking on the wrong entry, please don't try to correct it. Our entries come from tracking data, and will need to be corrected by operators.

  2. Do not remove vehicles from our list using the "remove from list" feature because they havent tracked (in a while), because they are on sale (or advertised for), or just because they were in an accident.

  3. Do not change a vehicles livery because its sanded down or is "being painted", please share a picture of the painted vehicle to update it.

  4. You should supply evidence, such as a picture you've uploaded to Imgur or a link to your evidence. However, please dont share evidence on private facebook groups as we cant see them, provide a picture of the post instead.

  5. Do not send edits to leave messages, please contact us on our discord instead.

  6. Give people the benefit of the doubt – if they have a track record of being reliable, it's OK if they haven't provided evidence. Don't undo/decline someone else's changes unless you're sure they're wrong.

Users can report content which they believe to be harmful, illegal, or or otherwise unsuitable. If we deem your content to be harmful, illegal, or otherwise unsuitable, we will permanently delete that content, and may ban your account from adding or editing content.

FAQ

Why was my edit disapproved?

To limit chaos, some users can approve/disapprove others' contributions. (This doesn't make them special or "admins" – there's no such thing!) I know some of the rules above are onerous, and they aren't written in stone, but if your edit was disapproved it probably broke one of them.

How do I add a vehicle?

Simply join our discord and request in the brute add requests channel, and our team will add them when they are free.

How do I add or edit a livery?

Sorry, only a few users can do that, you can request them in our discord.

I have a different question, or maybe a comment

Ask our team by email or the Discord server.
