Privacy policy

Last updated: 9 April 2025

timesbus.org (“this website”) is owned and operated by TimesBus (“we”).

You can use most of this website freely without sharing any personal details. But:

Using the “edit vehicle information” feature requires an email address, to facilitate logging in, and so that we can contact you if necessary. If you create an account and log in, we may also record your IP address, to help identify and prevent abuse and vandalism.

If you email us, we ask for your name and email address, so that we can reply. we may forward your message to e.g. a bus operating company or transport authority, if they are better placed to help you, unless you ask us not to.

If you ask us to, we can delete and/or provide you with a copy of your user account and/or any emails you‘ve sent us.
